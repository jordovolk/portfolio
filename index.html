Personal Information 

  •  My name is Jordan Volk, I grew up in Carver, MN all of my life and I am a video game fanatic.
  •  I recently discovered I was afraid of heights, during which I was 14,000 ft in the air about to jump out of a plane.
  •  I consider myself a person who likes participating in rather dangerous activities as long as they are not truly life threatening.
  •  A dream of mine would be to produce my own Xbox/Steam Game with a team of close friends on a development team.
  •  When I am not either stuck inside playing a game or jumping out of planes, I love travelling to South Dakota to Pheasant Hunt with my family.
  •  During my younger years I was heavily involved in Basketball and Baseball which helped me learn the importance of playing as a team and being a team player
